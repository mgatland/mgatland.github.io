Space Hawk

October 11, 2010

Space Hawk is an iPhone game by StarKiwi Studios. I was the programmer, working with designer\producer Ryan Thatcher and the artists from madMinute.

The game is no longer available, but you can still watch the trailer!

Space Hawk was displayed in the Dowse Art Museum and around New Zealand in the Arcade: Homegrown Video Games exhibition.
