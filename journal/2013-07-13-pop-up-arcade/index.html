Pop-up Indie Games Arcade!

July 13, 2013

Gather is an unconference held in Auckland every year.

This year, Alice, Amie and I used ten laptops to set up a pop-up arcade.

Thank you to everyone who came! We hope you had fun and saw something new. Super thank you to everyone who loaned their computer.

All of the games were made by Auckland developers. The games were:

  • The Kiwis’s Tale – Erik Hogan & Stephen Knightly
  • Robofortress – Joshua Smyth
  • Attack of the Meeplings – Joshua Smyth
  • Tide – Matthew Gatland
  • Cum Die with Me – Lianne Booton (Windows)
  • You Have To Go Down the Hole – Sergio Cornaga (Windows)
  • Worker and Parasite – Sergio Cornaga (Windows)
  • Noah more Heroes – Andrew Young, Anthony Lau, Erik Hogan, Michael Schaeffers, Sergio Cornaga (Windows)
  • Space Octopus Mono – Matthew Gatland (Windows, requires 1, 2 and maybe 3)
  • An unreleased game by Jonathan Rogers and Matthew Gatland.
