About Loose Leaf Games

Loose Leaf Games formed in December 2005. Matthew and Alice, a brother and sister team, decided to combine their talents and start producing great games to entertain people all over the world.

We are working on many different types of games instead of choosing one style straight away. Puzzle, action, and adventure games are some of the projects we have planned. We try to make games that are fun for both experienced gamers and for people who only play occasionally.

We are dedicated to our customers. You are the reason we can afford to spend our time doing what we love - making games. That's why we have a money back guarantee on all of our products. We will also attempt to solve any problems you have; simply contact us.
