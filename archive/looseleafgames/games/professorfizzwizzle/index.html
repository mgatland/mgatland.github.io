Professor Fizzwizzle

Professor Fizzwizzle needs your help! His once-friendly Friend-Bots have gone in to rage mode, and have thrown him out of his lab! Do you have what it takes to get past the Rage-Bots and bring the professor back to his lab?

We recommend Professor Fizzwizzle because of its clever puzzles and fun cartoon-like style. Once you try it, you'll want to play the whole game. As well as the normal puzzles, there are easy puzzles for children, and hard puzzles for experts.
