Privacy Policy at Loose Leaf Games

This page describes the policies of the Loose Leaf Games website with regards to information gathering.

Information Logging

Visitor's IP addresses are automatically recorded for security reasons. A user's IP address will not be shared with any other party unless the user is behaving in a potentially malicious way, in which case the IP address may be disclosed in order to protect LooseLeafGames.com and our customers.

Cookies

The Loose Leaf Games website may create or access cookies on visitor's computers to help us offer a better user experience and collect non-personal traffic data.

Registration Forms

The Loose Leaf Games includes some forms which allow visitors to enter information to subscribe to our newsletter and for other purposes. Information gathered from these forms will not be sold or given away. Email addresses collected from the newsletter sign-up form will be used solely to distribute our newsletter.

Information collected during a sale, including credit card information, will not be used for any purpose other than completing the sale. Sales are handled by our secure payment provider, Plimus.

External Links

Loose Leaf Games may link to third party websites. Loose Leaf Games is not responsible for the content of any website that is not a part of the LooseLeafGames.com domain.

Contact Information

If you have any queries about our privacy policy, please contact us using the Contact page.
