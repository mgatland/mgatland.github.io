<TAKEDOWN>
In the year 2170, man has finally triumphed. With the combined power of the entire world, the UNITY has ended famine, disease and war.
But some resist. Hungry for power, factions have formed to throw mankind back into conflict.
Stall have declared themselves independent from the UNITY and are arming themselves with illegal equipment.
Peace is no longer an option. The rebel uprising must be taken down.

Arrow keys — Move
W, A, S, D — Shoot in different directions
Shift — hold down while firing to use secondary fire mode
X — Show Notes
Esc — Main menu
Damage taken is displayed as a green bar.
Your weapon's heat is shown to the right. Firing too rapidly or taking damage will make it overheat. While your weapon is above 100% heat it will not be able to fire.
On the bottom left of the screen you may be given information helping you to complete the mission.
Avoiding projectiles is an important skill. When dodging, concentrate on the area around yourself and carefully move out of the way of incoming projectiles.
