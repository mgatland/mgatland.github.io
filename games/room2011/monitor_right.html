Room Tour

Upgrading to two monitors changed my life. I bought this monitor in 2009 for $25 on Trademe; it was my second greatest Trademe trade of all time.
Picking it up was interesting. It was in a tower in the city center. "Corporate Finance, Level 15 - just ring the bell in reception and ask for me. " I felt very underdressed in the elevator (men in suits) and at reception (women in black). Once I found my contact person, and recieved the monitor inside a very beaten cardboard box, I felt comfortable again. I'm comfortable carrying beaten cardboard boxes.
Go back.
